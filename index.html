How To Make a Chick-Fil-A Order

Materials You Will need

  • Gloves
  • Different Size Chich-Fil-A Bag
  • Cups for Drinks and/or Milkshakes
  • Sauces and napkins
  • Lids and straws
  • Dressings for salad
  • Utensils

Basic Steps

  1. Take the order from the guest and put if its carry out or dine in, ask for drinks then sauces then if done take payment. Move to step 2.
  2. At the Baging station look at the order on the screan and see what the entre and sides are. Once you have that figured out you place the order in the bag of the coresponding size. Move to step 3.
  3. At the Drink station look at the screen for the drinks if it is a milkshake or forested drink move to step 4. If it is just a fouintain drink, or coffe, or a bottled/boxed drink make it move to step 5.
  4. At the Shakes station look at the screen and make the coresponding shake. Move to step 5.
  5. At the Sauce station look at the sticker and/or screen and put the requested sauces into the bag. Move to step 6.
  6. Grab the drinks and the bagged order and call the name on the order or go to the table with the Table marker on the sticker.
  7. Repeat for the next guest.
